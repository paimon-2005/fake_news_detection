About TruthSense

A mission to fight digital misinformation with transparency and technology.

The Challenge

In an era of rapid information sharing, the spread of "fake news" has become a significant societal challenge. Misinformation can influence elections, affect public health, and cause unnecessary panic.

TruthSense was built to provide a data-driven layer of verification for online content, helping users distinguish between genuine reporting and manipulated narratives.

Our Technology

The system utilizes a Passive Aggressive Classifier, a type of online learning algorithm. Unlike many classifiers, it remains passive when a prediction is correct and makes aggressive updates only when a mistake occurs, making it ideal for large-scale textual data.

  • Natural Language Processing: Article text is converted to numerical vectors using TF-IDF.
  • Dynamic Learning: The model can be retrained as new datasets become available.
  • Linguistic Markers: Analysis includes sentiment, vocabulary diversity, and structural patterns.
